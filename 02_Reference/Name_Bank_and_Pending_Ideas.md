## Names that may need polish later

- Asterweald: good meaning, maybe slightly direct. Alternatives: Asterwald, Asterveil, Astermere, Shiroaster, Eirastel.
- Hazelmour: good but may need stronger ash flavor if it becomes too soft. Alternatives: Orenmoure, Ashenmour, Hazeria, Crimson Marches.
- Klazen: strong but modern-sounding. Could stay.
- Astrolia: understandable but slightly obvious. Alternatives: Astraea, Starhaven Isles, Celestara, Meridia.

## Pending worldbuilding questions

1. Is the Aster Forest a true nation, a sacred territory, or a land that other nations incorrectly mark as a nation?
2. Does Lyseria come from Asterweald openly, or is her origin hidden even from most people?
3. Was the ash disaster in Hazelmour caused by Old Orenia, by an outside faction, or by a misunderstood Asterweald relic?
4. Is Vivienne from a hunter family, a noble family, an orphaned frontier settlement, or an order/guild?
5. Are the gods real individuals, cultural myths, spirit-class beings, or something more ambiguous?
6. How advanced should Klazen be compared with the rest of the world?
7. Is Namarion neutral, corrupt, or simply practical?
8. Does the map have one main continent plus islands, or several continents?
