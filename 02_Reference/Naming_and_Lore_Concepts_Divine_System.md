Other possible names:

- The First Root
- The Aster Root

## Divine recognition terms

### Divine Act

An extraordinary achievement that causes a god to notice someone.

### Divine Invitation

A physical invitation sent by a god after a Divine Act.

### Root Permission

Permission from the Crownroot or Rootwarden for outliers who do not belong to a single divine domain.

### Forsworn

Current strongest term for someone who accepted or was offered a divine path but rejected, abandoned, or defected from it.

## Related person-status terms

Possible supporting vocabulary:

- **Unanswered** — someone who ignored or refused an invitation before entering a domicile
- **Forsworn** — someone who rejected or abandoned a divine path
- **Severed** — someone whose divine bond was forcibly cut
- **Fallen** — someone expelled from a domicile
- **Unclaimed** — someone no god can properly categorize
- **Rootmarked** — someone recognized by the Crownroot or Rootwarden instead of a single god
