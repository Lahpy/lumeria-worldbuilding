# Old Notes Conversion Guide

This guide explains how the older Avalon notes are being reused.

## Keep as major nations

| Old Note | New Use |
|---|---|
| E'l Ancalen | Major nation of formal magic and divine light. |
| Hazelmour | Major ash-frontier nation, expanded with Athea and Old Orenia material. |
| Namarion | Major canal city-state/trade nation. |
| Klazen | Major technology/magical-engineering nation. |
| Luminor | Major art/trade/cultural nation. |
| Truo Crua | Major underground republic. |
| Astrolia | Major star-island nation. |

## Merge into other nations

| Old Note | New Use |
|---|---|
| Alheim | Reworked into Asterweald atmosphere and landmarks: Shimmervale, Aster Tree, Spirit Valley. |
| Sylphora | Reworked into a forest settlement within Asterweald. |
| Botanical Log | Idea bank for Asterweald flora, rare plants, and magical ecology. |
| Athea | Reworked into Hazelmour's hunter/blacksmith/adventurer side. |
| Old Orenia | Reworked into Hazelmour's ruined inner territory and Tenebris conflict zone. |
| Mosmaria | Reworked into a rose city/province inside Luminor. |
| Othorion | Reworked into Klazen's capital city. |
| Aurorion / Zephyria ideas | Reworked into Klazen's Saphera/floating-city/airship side. |

## Demote to later expansion

| Old Note | Possible Future Use |
|---|---|
| Caradia | Peaceful meadow province, wind-magic countryside, refuge zone, or Luminor/E'l Ancalen neighbor. |
| Endor | Military border state, bank/fortress nation, or northern defense power. |
| Eschburg | Grey-rock mountain region, quarry country, or Truo Crua surface neighbor. |
| Evaria | Nomadic people, caravan culture, horse archers, steppe routes. |
| Hyrulea / Oasisia | Desert/nomadic expansion material. |
| Marintha | Sea-folk expansion material near Namarion or Astrolia. |
| Aetheria | Dimensional mystery material, probably late-story. |
| Vulcania | Forge/volcano expansion material if more weapon lore is needed. |
| Xanthea | Witch/seer culture, possibly rival to E'l Ancalen or Astrolia. |

## Key rewrite decisions

### E'l Ancalen
Old: birthplace of magic.  
New: birthplace of formal, recorded, institutional magic.

### Alheim
Old: sacred land of gods.  
New: absorbed into Asterweald as sacred landmarks and edge settlements, so the Aster Forest can become the emotional/mystic center.

### Hazelmour / Athea / Old Orenia
Old: three separate concepts.  
New: one major region with living autumn culture, hunter cities, and ash-ruined territory.

### Klazen / Othorion / Saphera
Old: overlapping innovation/floating city ideas.  
New: one major machine nation with a research capital and floating metropolis.

### Luminor / Mosmaria
Old: art nation and rose kingdom.  
New: one stronger cultural/trade nation with Mosmaria as a rose city/province.
