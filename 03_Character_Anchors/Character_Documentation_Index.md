## Core integration rule

The nation structure gives Lumeria a map.

The character documentation gives Lumeria its emotional and thematic center.

Do not let the world become a generic fantasy atlas. The most important worldbuilding questions should continue to orbit:

- What does civilization misunderstand about magic?
- Why does the Asterweald feel older than ordinary history?
- Why is Lyseria called The White Fox instead of simply being understood as Lyseria?
- Why is Vivienne called The Ash Huntress, and what does that title cost her?
- How do myths, names, titles, and private intimacy conflict with one another?
