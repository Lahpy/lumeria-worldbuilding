# Quick Character Cards

## Lyseria Asterveil

**Title:** The White Fox  
**Home:** The Aster Forest / Asterweald  
**Primary Magic:** White Magic  
**Combat Role:** Mage first, sword user second  
**Core Theme:** Knowledge becoming power  
**Public Image:** Saint, miracle worker, terrifying mage, woman from beyond the Aster Forest  
**Private Identity:** Quiet, thoughtful, forest-raised, still learning how to live among people

### Core identity

Lyseria reveals what things truly are.

She understands magic beneath categories, bodies beneath flesh, the Spirit Layer beneath the body, and the Core beneath the Spirit Layer.

Her legend is frightening because she is not merely powerful. She is comprehending.

## Vivienne Valemont

**Title:** The Ash Huntress  
**Origin:** Low-ranking noble house / House Valemont  
**Primary Magic:** Fire Magic  
**Combat Role:** Swordswoman and mythical creature hunter  
**Core Theme:** Human resolve becoming the predator of the impossible  
**Public Image:** Noble-born monster hunter whose reputation is respected by frontier towns, hunters, and people who understand mythical creatures  
**Private Identity:** Warm, restrained, disciplined, human, and emotionally guarded

### Core identity

Vivienne ends what should not be able to end.

She studies mythical creatures, understands their habits and weaknesses, and uses disciplined fire through her sword to make impossible wounds permanent.

Her legend is frightening because she is the answer to creatures that believed they had no predator.

## Shared relationship function

Lyseria reveals.  
Vivienne finishes.

Lyseria understands the structure.  
Vivienne strikes the decisive point.

To the world, they are The White Fox and The Ash Huntress.  
To each other, they are Lyseria and Vivienne.
