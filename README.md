# Lumeria Worldbuilding Notes

This folder contains the current rewritten worldbuilding notes for the setting now using **Lumeria** as the working world name.

The notes are organized around a map-ready nation structure rather than the older broad Avalon-style atlas. The current structure keeps the useful old city and region names, but rebuilds the setting around the newer direction: the Asterweald, White Magic, Lyseria Asterveil, Vivienne Valemont, mythical creature hunting, ash/frontier regions, and civilization trying to classify things it does not fully understand.

## Folder structure

- `00_Overview/` - world premise, map skeleton, and world-name decision notes.
- `01_Nations/` - one file per major nation or major map region.
- `02_Reference/` - city index, old-note conversion guide, name bank, and source archive inventory.
- `03_Character_Anchors/` - character-world integration notes and extracted character documentation.
- `04_Systems_and_Lore/` - magic system, Awakening system, mythical creatures, and title/name motifs.
- `05_Relationship_and_Story/` - Lyseria/Vivienne relationship notes and future arc seeds.
- `99_Source_Documents/` - original uploaded/reference documents kept for traceability.

## Current core direction

**Lumeria** is a high-fantasy world of ancient forests, luminous magic kingdoms, ash-scarred frontiers, canal cities, underground republics, star islands, and machine-lit towers.

At its heart is the **Asterweald**, an ancient forest territory tied to White Magic, fox spirits, and hidden truths older than formal civilization.

The two main character anchors are:

- **Lyseria Asterveil**, The White Fox - a White Magic user tied to the Asterweald, truth, origin, structure, and terrifying comprehension.
- **Vivienne Valemont**, The Ash Huntress - a noble-born mythical creature hunter tied to fire, ash, survival, precision, and finality.

The world should remain beautiful, mysterious, and dangerous, with enough clear nations and cities to eventually support a visually rich map.
