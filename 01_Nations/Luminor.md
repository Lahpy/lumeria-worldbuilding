# Luminor

## Role in the world

Luminor is the sunlit cultural, artistic, diplomatic, and trade nation. It keeps the old Luminor identity but can absorb Mosmaria as a rose-city/province.

It gives the world beauty, luxury, diplomacy, railway travel, ports, high culture, and social pressure.

## Core identity

- Art and craftsmanship
- Sunlit ports and trade routes
- Beaux-Arts architecture
- Gardens and imported plant life
- Wealth, refinement, diplomacy
- High society and cultural soft power

## Visual language

- Large glass windows
- Wide boulevards
- White and cream stone
- Sunlit plazas
- Art markets and galleries
- Rose gardens and pearl-white districts
- Grand railway stations
- Elegant temples and museums

## Government / authority

Luminor can keep a democratic system with a prime minister, but the rewrite should emphasize that wealthy patrons, artist guilds, port authorities, and diplomatic families have major influence.

Old note to preserve:

- Prime Minister Orberich can remain as a name, though the role can be revised later.

## Religion / culture

Athena, Goddess of Art, can remain as the patron of artistic creation and culture. The Temple of Athena in Lapula City Center should stay.

Mosmaria's Alhoris, Goddess of Roses, can become a regional cult, festival figure, or local patron within Luminor.

## Cities and locations

### Lapula
Capital of Luminor. A major port city with grand streets, markets, art halls, galleries, and official buildings.

### Passage De Lumiere Grand Railway Station
A major landmark and transit point. It should connect Luminor to inland nations and possibly to Truo Crua's underground routes.

### Temple of Athena
Located in Lapula City Center. A major cultural and religious landmark.

### Mosmaria
Rewritten from a separate rose kingdom into a city, province, or noble garden-state within Luminor's sphere. Pearl-white buildings, red roses, music, dance, pastries, jewelry, tapestries, and romance-focused festivals.

### Rosecourt Palace
Possible new landmark in Mosmaria. Used for diplomatic receptions, art patronage, and noble politics.

## Economy

- Trade
- Art and craftsmanship
- Ceramics and textiles
- Luxury goods
- Ports and railways
- Cultural tourism
- Diplomatic hosting
- Imported plant cultivation

## Conflicts

- Luminor may publicly support peace while privately buying relics through Namarion.
- Cultural elites may romanticize Asterweald without understanding its danger.
- Mosmaria's beauty can hide class pressure and noble control.
- Luminor's wealth makes it influential even when it avoids direct war.
