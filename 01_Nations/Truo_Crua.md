# Truo Crua

## Role in the world

Truo Crua is the underground republic and transport nation. It is unique enough to keep as a major nation because it adds vertical geography to the map.

It gives the world tunnels, subterranean settlements, hill homes, rail systems, stone culture, and underground political neutrality.

## Core identity

- Underground republic
- Subterranean cities
- Hill homes and cave districts
- Massive transport stations
- Stonework, mining, tunnels, trade routes
- Cultural melting pot beneath the surface

## Visual language

- Warm lantern-lit tunnels
- Carved stone halls
- Underground rail lines
- Stalactite architecture
- Terraced cave settlements
- Surface hill doors leading into large interiors
- Bronze signs, tunnel maps, stone bridges

## Government / authority

Truo Crua should remain a republic. Its government can be built around city representatives, transport guilds, mining families, and underground safety councils.

Possible governing body: **The Deep Assembly**.

## Cities and locations

### Midrel
Capital of Truo Crua. An underground city built around a massive central cavern and transport hub.

### Midrel Frisker Transport Station
Major landmark. This should be one of the most important travel nodes in the world.

### The Frisker Line
Underground transport route connecting distant nations. It can run beneath mountains, borders, and dangerous terrain.

### The Sedimentary Border
The old note's idea can be developed into the boundary between surface jurisdiction and underground Truo Cruan territory.

### Hill Settlements
Surface-access villages built into hillsides. Outsiders may think these are small villages until they realize each one connects to a vast underground network.

## Economy

- Transport
- Tunnels and engineering
- Mining
- Stonework
- Safe passage contracts
- Underground markets
- Refuge and neutral meeting spaces

## Conflicts

- Every nation wants access to Truo Crua's transport lines.
- Smugglers use old tunnel branches.
- Some sealed routes may lead near Asterweald roots or Old Orenia ruins.
- Truo Crua must stay neutral while carrying goods, refugees, weapons, relics, and secrets.
